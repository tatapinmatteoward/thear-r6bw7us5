## Why Lattice Tops?
Lattice tops aren't just beautiful—they allow steam to escape during baking, preventing soggy bottoms and ensuring even cooking. With PieCraft Pro's precision guides and customizable templates, you can weave flawless patterns without the frustration.

### Step-by-Step Guide Using PieCraft Pro
1. **Prepare Your Dough**: Roll out your dough to the perfect thickness using our digital caliper feature. Aim for 1/8 inch for optimal flexibility.

2. **Design Your Pattern**: In the PieCraft Pro app, select from pre-loaded lattice templates or create your own. Adjust weave density for a tight or open design—perfect for showcasing fruit fillings.

3. **Cut and Weave**: Use the app's AR overlay to guide your cuts. For an advanced twist, try the diagonal lattice variation: cut strips at 45-degree angles for a modern, diamond-effect top.

4. **Assembly Tips**: Chill your lattice strips for 15 minutes before weaving to maintain shape. PieCraft Pro's timer reminds you, and our video tutorials walk you through the process.

## Pro Tips for Perfection
- **Flavor Pairings**: Pair a classic apple pie with a cinnamon-spiced lattice for aroma that wafts through the kitchen.
- **Customization**: Experiment with colored doughs or add edible flowers between weaves for an Instagram-worthy dessert.
- **Troubleshooting**: If your lattice breaks, PieCraft Pro's repair mode suggests quick fixes, like patching with dough scraps.
