Grill it, roast it, braise it, or fry it—its versatility knows no bounds. From Asian-inspired stir-fries to Southern-style barbecue, pork belly adapts to any cuisine.

## Beginner-Friendly Pork Belly Recipe: Crispy Oven-Baked Bliss

Ready to try it yourself? Here's our go-to recipe for crispy pork belly that's easy and delicious.

### Ingredients (Serves 4):
- 2 lbs pork belly, skin-on
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika

### Instructions:
1. **Prep the Pork**: Pat the pork belly dry and score the skin in a diamond pattern. Rub with oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika. Let it sit at room temperature for 30 minutes.
2. **Bake Low and Slow**: Preheat your oven to 300°F (150°C). Place the pork on a wire rack over a baking sheet and roast for 2 hours until tender.
3. **Crisp It Up**: Increase the heat to 450°F (230°C) and roast for another 20-30 minutes until the skin is golden and crispy.
4. **Rest and Serve**: Let it rest for 10 minutes, then slice and enjoy with your favorite sides—think roasted veggies or a fresh salad.

Pro Tip: For extra crunch, blast it under the broiler for the last few minutes!
